The solar system is home to 8 planets, approximately 170 natural planetary satellites (moons), and countless asteroids, meteorites, and comets. Found at the centre of the solar system and influencing the motion of all the other bodies through its gravitationally force is the Sun, which in itself contains more than 99 percent of the mass of the system.
